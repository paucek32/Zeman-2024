Custom Manufacturing

At Zeman Tool & Manufacturing, we specialize in providing custom manufacturing solutions tailored to your specific needs. Our state-of-the-art equipment and experienced team ensure precision and quality in every project. In order to react quickly to market demands with the highest quality, we offer a very diverse group of machine tools and support equipment, including over 48 pieces of CNC equipment!

Our Machines


5-Axis Machine

5-Axis Machine

This is our 5-axis machining center. It has a swivel table for 5-axis simultaneous machining, high work piece weight limits, a large tool magazine, and an 18,000 rpm spindle.

Boring Mills

Boring Machines

Zeman’s boring mills service projects from basic high speed material removal to precision component machining. All of Zeman’s boring mills are equipped with full B axis rotary tables and offer precision 3-D machining capabilities.

Vertical Milling

Turning Services

Turning Services

We offer advanced turning services for a wide range of projects, from small components to large, complex parts. Our modern equipment and skilled team handle diverse sizes and specifications, ensuring precision and quality in every piece.

High-Speed Machining

High-Speed Machining

Zeman has several high-speed capability machines. With 3D functionality, linear liquid cooled drives, 42,000 rpm spindles, balanced shrink fit holders, laser pre-setters, and online cameras provide remote monitors for around-the-clock machining. With these high rpm’s and cutters down to .005” diameter, Zeman can cut the finest details.

Wire EDM

Wire EDM

Multiple Electrical Discharge Machining (EDM) are capable of wire cutting precise and accurate compound angles on a variety of platform materials.

Grinding Machines

Grinding Services

Zeman utilizes premium grinding equipment to achieve: high tolerance, surface finishing, and flatness requirements. Our capabilities include jig, surface, and cylindrical grinding that achieve a level of precision other tooling companies cannot match.

Cylindrical Grinding Machines

Cylindrical Grinding Services

Utilizing cylindrical grinding machines, we are able to grind workpieces in individual, small batch and high volume production.
