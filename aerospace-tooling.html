Aerospace Tooling

At Zeman Tool & MFG, we specialize in precision CNC machining of advanced aerospace alloys—materials engineered to perform under the most demanding conditions. Our experience with these metals allows us to produce components that maintain exceptional strength, accuracy, and reliability even in extreme environments.

Our climate-controlled facility and state-of-the-art CNC equipment ensure dimensional stability and consistency when working with materials known for their toughness and heat resistance. From prototype to production, we apply proven machining strategies to meet tight tolerances and critical aerospace specifications.

Materials We Work With

We machine a wide range of high-performance alloys, including:

  • Aluminum Alloys (2024, 6061, 7075): Lightweight and strong—ideal for aircraft structures and housings.
  • Titanium Alloys (Ti-6Al-4V, Ti-5553): Exceptional strength-to-weight ratio and corrosion resistance for engine and airframe parts.
  • Nickel-Based Superalloys (Inconel 718, 625): High-temperature strength for turbine and propulsion components.
  • Stainless Steels (17-4 PH, 15-5 PH): Durable and corrosion-resistant for mechanical and support systems

Why Aerospace Manufacturers Choose Zeman

Precision and attention to detail are the cornerstones of our manufacturing philosophy. In the aerospace sector, where there is zero margin for error, we dedicate ourselves to achieving the highest levels of accuracy and quality. Our state-of-the-art facilities utilize advanced CNC machinery, precision measuring equipment, and stringent quality control processes to ensure every component meets exact specifications. Our skilled craftsmen and engineers meticulously oversee each stage of production, guaranteeing that every detail is perfected. This unwavering commitment to excellence ensures that our clients receive reliable, high-performance parts that stand up to the most demanding aerospace applications.
