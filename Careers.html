Engineering Internship

Employment Type: Internship

Job Type: Engineer

Job Description:

At Zeman, we strive to produce high quality, custom mechanical tooling solutions for multiple areas of industries. As an intern, you will be immersed in our fast-paced and team-oriented environment with our mentors. They have decades of experience and a passion to share their expertise with the next generation. You could be working on automotive parts one day and rocket parts the next! Come Join our team and experience job diversity daily!

Responsibilities:

  • Generate 2D manufacturing drawings from 3D models
  • Assist in the design and development of multiple mechanical components
  • Provides mechanical engineering support for new design development
  • Work directly with 2D/3D CAD models for conceptual and final designs of custom tooling
  • Preform product testing inspection and documentation
  • Research and information gathering for upcoming projects

Requirements:

  • Willingness to learn new techniques, methods and functions
  • Mechanical aptitude
  • Self-responsibility
  • Strong attention to detail
  • Reliability
  • Critical thinking and problem-solving skills

Perks & Benefits:

  • Opportunities for a full-time job offer at the end of your internship
  • Casual/informal dress environment
  • Networking opportunities across multiple large industries
  • Flexible work schedule to fit around school and sport obligations
  • Safe, clean and temperature-controlled environment
