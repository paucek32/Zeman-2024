Workholding Fixtures

Zeman focuses on the design and manufacturing of workholding fixtures to assist in the production of machined parts. Custom workholding fixtures we manufacture range from basic to complex, and are made for multiple industries.

Zeman Tool produces 4 and 5-axis workholding fixtures for mass production of machined parts in a time-efficient and cost-effective manner. We serve various industries including automotive, medical, industrial, and aviation. We accept challenges from any industry and focus on meeting their requirements.

Tombstone Fixtures

Specializing in both weldments and castings for tombstones, Zeman offers custom fabrication to fit tooling needs.

Zeman workholding tombstones can be cast from in-stock patterns in different grades of cast iron, or fabricated and machined to any specific configurations. Fast turnarounds are offered for custom fabrication of tombstone weldments based on those requirements.
